Rules of The Wizards' Chronicles

Do note: In not following any of the rules listed here, you will be subject to punishment.


  • Do not do anything that may be considered spam, whether it be via the OOC channel, emote, say, certain spells, etc.
  • No Multikeying.
  • Do not abuse any bugs you may encounter. PM any GM (the people with colored names) - Include details of how to recreate the bug. This includes but is not limited to:
    • Hurting/Killing someone in a safe zone by any trick you may find.
    • Moving someone from a safe zone to a kill zone for any exploit, if they are inside, leave them there!
  • Overly offensive comments, such those involving race, gender, sexual preference, etc, are not allowed.
  • Do not use spells or any other method to block off areas to prevent others from accessing them.
  • No one is to have their masks on during a class/event.
  • Do not use any scripts or third-party programs that give you an advantage over another player.
  • Do not advertise other games, servers, etc.

If, for whatever reason, a sibling who uses the same IP address as you is banned from playing this game, we are not to blame. We will not revert any ban affecting one user due to something that their sibling did.
